About me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the College of Information Engineering, Zhejiang University of Technology, supervised by Prof. Defeng He and Prof. Xiaoqin Zhang (IEEE Senior Member). I am also a visiting student at the Imaging Systems Laboratory (ISL), The University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Edmund Y. Lam (IEEE Fellow). My research focuses on multimodal information fusion and perception, with an emphasis on efficient deployment on embedded systems for real-world applications.
